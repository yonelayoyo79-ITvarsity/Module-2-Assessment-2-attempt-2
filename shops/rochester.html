Rochester

At Rochester, we believe that your home should be as individual as you are. As such, we have searched far and wide to curate the most expertly crafted collections of stylish furniture and décor items for every room in your home. Whether you are looking for ultra-modern design, or something more traditional – Rochester has something to suit your needs.
Since opening its doors in 1994, Rochester’s aim has always been to put their customers first by providing them with timeless, elegant furniture that boasts top-end quality at an affordable price. We’ve achieved this by specialising in functionality, timeless design and superior customer service.
